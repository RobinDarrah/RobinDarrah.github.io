About me

A Video Games industry professional with years of experience in Indie Publishing, Pitching, Production, Mentoring, Career Advice and Diversity Advocacy.

Having worked with over 100 Publishers and Developers I have vast knowledge of the industry specialising in Commercial best practices with an friendly and accessible approach to consulting.

I’ve reviewed over 1000 game pitches and know the pitching process well from both perspectives. I can offer a range of advice to compliment your creative vision.

My latest passion is the educational side of the industry sharing knowledge, instilling confidence and lifting up talented individuals from under represented groups. I’ve previously worked with Games London, Gi Academy, Next Gen Skills Academy, The Swiss She Got Game Program, Safe in Our World, Tiga and Game Academy.

I have also featured on many panel’s and podcasts including at the GDC Conference, First Playable Conference, Playable Futures Podcast and Games London.

I’m also a mum and carer to two lovely sons, both with a passion for gaming, and one of whom has Autism.

Please get in touch if you’d like to talk further with me and if there’s anything I can do for you or your team.
